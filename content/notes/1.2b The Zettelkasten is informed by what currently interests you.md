The Zettelkasten is informed by what currently interests you. By capturing
fleeting and reference notes into the system, it fuels what you will use to
create main notes. Which informs the material you want to write about.

> All of the loops come back to reinforcing what we find interesting in the world,
> influencing the ideas we capture, refine, connect, and search for in our
> zettelkasten. <a href="#citeproc_bib_item_1">[1]</a>
