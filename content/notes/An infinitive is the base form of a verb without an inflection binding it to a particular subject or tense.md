An infinitive is the base form of a verb without an inflection binding it to a
particular subject or tense. In English this commonly presents it self as a verb
preceded by "to", other languages like Spanish and Slovak have a single word
that represents the action. For example, "to read" is "čítať" in Slovak,
expressed in a single word.
