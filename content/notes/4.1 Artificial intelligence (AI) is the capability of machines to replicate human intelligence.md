Artificial intelligence (AI) is the capability of machines to perform tasks that
require intelligence. AI is a deep field of study that started in the 1950s; in
the 2020s, there has been an AI boom due to advances in generative AI.
Generative AI is not the only form of AI, and it might not lead us closer to
artificial general intelligence (AGI).
