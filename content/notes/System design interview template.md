The system design interview template is from "System Design Interview" by Alex
Xu, and consists of four steps to guide interviewees through designing a system.

The fours steps are:


## Step 1 - Understand the problem and establish design scope {#step-1-understand-the-problem-and-establish-design-scope}

Ask clarifying questions, get all of the requisite informatioe.


## Step 2 - Propose high-level design and get buy-in {#step-2-propose-high-level-design-and-get-buy-in}

Create initial blueprint and get help from the interviewer. Draw diagrams, and
do necessary calculations.


## Step 3 - Design deep dive {#step-3-design-deep-dive}

Focus on areas that the interviewer wants. Elaborate where needed, and make
improvments on your high level design. Iterate.


## Step 4 - Wrap up {#step-4-wrap-up}

Everything can be improved upon. Give a recap of design. Say what areas could be
a problem/bottleneck.
