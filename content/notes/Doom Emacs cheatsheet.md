Here are some of the commands that I find myself using most in Doom Emacs.


## Keybinds {#keybinds}


### Follow org link {#follow-org-link}

C-c C-o


### Reload Config {#reload-config}

space h r r


### Find notes {#find-notes}

space n r f


### Open backlinks {#open-backlinks}

space n r r


### Insert structure template {#insert-structure-template}

C-c C-,


### Org Agenda (open next 10 day view) {#org-agenda--open-next-10-day-view}

space o A a (n)

I actually like the n better since it gives the agenda and non-scheduled TODOs


### Schedule something {#schedule-something}

C-c C-s


### Check box toggle {#check-box-toggle}

C-c C-c


### Toggle Zen {#toggle-zen}

space t z


### Open Daily {#open-daily}

space n r d t


### Swtich to project {#swtich-to-project}

space p p


### Add new project {#add-new-project}

space p a


### Exporting menu (dispatch) {#exporting-menu--dispatch}

space m e


### Getting undo tree (vundo) {#getting-undo-tree--vundo}

space s u


### Create or go to yesterday's daily note {#create-or-go-to-yesterday-s-daily-note}

space n r d y


## Magit {#magit}


### Stage files {#stage-files}

s


### Commit {#commit}

c c


### Push {#push}

p p
