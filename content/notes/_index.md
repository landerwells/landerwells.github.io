reconstruction. 

For a while I published notes directly from my Zettelkasten, with no additional
context. This approach did not provide the benefits I thought it might, and I
don't think it was all that interesting to read my personal notes. I have
learned from this experience. I believe a good medium is to post shorter form
content here, or things that I think would be good references for others or
myself.
