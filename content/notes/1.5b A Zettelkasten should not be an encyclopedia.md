A Zettelkasten should not be an encyclopedia (or Wikipedia). The purpose the
Zettelkasten is not to replicate knowledge that is readily available on the
internet or otherwise. Ideally, a Zettelkasten will contain many original
thoughts, along with facts or other pieces of information.


## see [Titles in a Zettelkasten should be declarative]({{< relref "1.6 Titles in a Zettelkasten should be declarative.md" >}}) {#see-titles-in-a-zettelkasten-should-be-declarative--1-dot-6-titles-in-a-zettelkasten-should-be-declarative-dot-md}

A trap Zettelkasten users (including myself) fall into is trying to title their
notes similarly to Wikipedia, with broad ambiguous titles. These are neither
atomic nor representative of what is stored inside the note.
